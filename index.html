Splendid Cyborg was founded in 2018 as a web and mobile app development consultancy serving savvy non-technical startup founders looking for a CTO-for-hire.

We had a successful run for three years building MVP’s and software engineering organizations for several early-stage startups.

Currently, Splendid Cyborg is reforming as a single-person vehicle for next-gen tech research conducted by its founder, Ersin Akinci.

Ersin’s vision is to address social and environmental issues through democratizing technology, whether that's in 3D printing, energy, housing, or finance.

The goal is to create a new startup focused on one of those new technologies, and to build a world-class team that's passionate about their potential.
